You will Experiment, Architect & Build AI solutions for learning at scale, focusing on compact, efficient models optimized for client-side, low-resource applications. You also have experience in creating evaluation datasets and metrics for fine-tuned, lightweight models.

You will create efficient prompt and chain flows, build reusable templates, and integrate models with client-side apps, APIs, or edge devices using FastAPI, Flask, or mobile deployment frameworks.

You will mentor junior engineers, guide them in efficient experimentation, lightweight model design, and rapid prototyping, and ensure production-ready, well-tested code with typing, pytest, packaging, and thorough documentation..
